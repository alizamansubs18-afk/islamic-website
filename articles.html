The Importance of Prayer

Prayer (Salah) is the foundation of Islamic life and the connection between a believer and Allah. Regular prayer strengthens faith, patience, and discipline.

Charity in Islam

Giving charity (Zakat and Sadaqah) purifies wealth and helps the needy. It also reminds us to be grateful and compassionate.
